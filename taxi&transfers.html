Taxi or Transfers

Speks Transportation Services provides professional and reliable door to door service to and from the airport, hotel or private residence. Our objective for transfer services from or to the Airport, is to seek your comfort and to be able to offer a personalized service tailored to suit your needs.

This are preconcerted services to any destination on the island of St.Maarten / St.Martin, our prices are fixed, and fully inclusive. Do not wait in the queue for a taxi, avoid problems if you travel with disabled people that require to travel with wheelchair, if you travel with kids, extra luggage, sports equipment…

Enjoy the convenience of having a driver who is waiting for you at the arrival door of your flight at the time agreed with the taxi that best suits your needs.
