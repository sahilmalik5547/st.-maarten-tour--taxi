ABOUT US

Speedy – Punctual – Economical – Kind – Service:

Is what “Speks” Taxi Service Stands for …….

Speks Transportation Services is operated by Fitzroy Gijsbertha. We primarily believe in getting you safely and comfortably to your destination, speedily in the sense of not letting you have any delays to your destination for anyone’s else’s personal matters.

Economical prices with VIP service is one of the reasons why Speks is Sint Maarten’s most renowned Transportation service provider; you get the service of a VIP for the prices stipulated by law, without running the risk of being overcharged.

Kindness is a must, personal and professional courtesy is always in place at Spek’s…our belief in kindness and courtesy

Speks Transportation Services isn’t merely a taxi service means of transportation; in fact Speks covers all areas of transportation, directly as well as indirectly. Reservations for VIP hook-ups, group tours, excursions, beach trips, shopping trips, casino trips and so much more are incorporated into “Speks” services.

Our taxi service is very quick and very responsive to your calls…not to mention at “Speks” we provide you with valuable information that you can use to make your vacation a very fruitful one…and rest assure you “will” want to return.

Our Vehicles

All of our vehicles offer an excellent ride, are extremely safe and reliable and are easy to get in and out of. They have oversized, tinted sightseeing windows and ample room for luggage.
